About Teacher's Day

Teacher's Day is a special day to honor and appreciate the hard work and dedication of teachers in shaping the future of students. It is celebrated on different dates worldwide, but the essence remains the same - to express gratitude to teachers for their invaluable contributions.

"A teacher affects eternity; they can never tell where their influence stops." - Henry Adams
